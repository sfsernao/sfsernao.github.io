About Us

CHIRP (Collective for Holistic Interdisciplinary Research in Photonics) is a research group dedicated to the study and development of advanced technologies in photonics, integrating interdisciplinary and holistic approaches. Beyond its research work, CHIRP is a formative and collaborative space where students can strengthen their passion for science, share innovative ideas and actively participate in projects that generate real impact. By joining CHIRP, members have the opportunity to grow academically and professionally in an environment that fosters creativity, teamwork and critical thinking.
